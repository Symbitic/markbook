Markbook-Specific Extensions

Markbook implements a number of extensions to CommonMark. These are intended to make the writing process more pleasant.

Superscript/Subscript

Markbook supports superscript and subscript syntax.

H~2~O

renders as: H2O

21^st^ Century

renders as: 21st Century

Definition Lists

Markbook supports definition lists, similar to the ones used by pandoc.

Term 1
            
            : Definition 1

renders as:

Term 1
Definition 1

Footnotes

Footnotes are another feature whose syntax is borrowed from pandoc.

Here is a footnote reference.[^1]
            
            [^1]: Here is the footnote.

renders as:

Here is a footnote reference.1
